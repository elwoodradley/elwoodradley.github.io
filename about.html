About

Stone Toad is one person. Day job keeps the lights on; this is the long game. The plan isn’t a viral weekend — it’s a catalog built slowly, over years, by someone who isn’t in a hurry and isn’t going anywhere.

I write the code, the words, and set up the machines myself — mostly Linux, Godot 4, a stack of laptops that have no business still working. The games tend toward quiet horror and deadpan comedy, often the same thing from two angles. If there’s a through-line, it’s attention paid to small wrong things until they turn into something.
