home games devlog writing about

One person makes everything here: the games, the words, the machines they run on. I keep the work small on purpose, games that respect your time, the way the ones I grew up on did. Built for people with kids asleep down the hall and ten minutes to themselves. Avid reader, sporadic writer. These are my hobbies, and I'm glad to share them with you.
